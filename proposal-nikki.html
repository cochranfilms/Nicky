Executive Summary

Build a high‑performance, AI‑powered platform that books you, educates your audience, and scales your content while you sleep.

  • Unified booking with automated scheduling and pre‑call workflows.
  • Education hub for workshops, courses, and premium resources.
  • AI content engine for reusable prompts, brand voice, and analytics.
Deliverable Themes
  • Conversion‑optimized website and funnels
  • Operational automations and CRM hygiene
  • Creator workflows and media systemization

Outcomes & KPIs

  • Increase qualified booking requests and close rate.
  • Reduce response and scheduling time with automation.
  • Grow consistent content output without burnout.
  • Improve course sales and workshop attendance.
  • Centralize analytics for revenue‑focused decisions.
